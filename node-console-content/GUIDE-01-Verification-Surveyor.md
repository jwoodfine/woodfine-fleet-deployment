# 🧭 GUIDE-01: Verification Surveyor Operations
**Operational Tier:** 3 (Fleet Deployment)
**Target Node:** node-console-content

## I. OPERATIONAL MANDATE
This console acts as the primary human ingestion gateway for Woodfine Management Corp. Operators must use this terminal to clear the personnel `discovery-queue` generated by the PointSav harvesting engines.

## II. EXECUTION PROTOCOL
1. Open your terminal.
2. Execute the global command: `pointsav-surveyor`
3. The terminal will present a single "Index Card" containing unverified identity data.
4. **Air-Gap Step:** Open your web browser and search for the individual to verify their current employment status.
5. Paste their verified LinkedIn URL into the terminal.
6. When prompted, select the correct Ontological Archetype (e.g., `1` for The Executive) based on their current role.
7. Repeat until the terminal locks you out for the day (Maximum 10).
